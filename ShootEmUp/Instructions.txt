Instructions for Bullet Hell Game:

Controls: 
W - move up
A - move left
S - move down
D - move right

V - player shoots bullets

Hit Points (HP):
Player - 5 lives
Enemy - 3 lives 

Points System:
For every wave of enemies you survive, you gain 1 point. 
For every enemy you kill, you gain 5 points. 
In each wave, there is only one enemy that is capable of being killed. 

Goal:
Keep dodging all the bullets coming your way, try to kill enemies for extra points, and continue playing until you lose all 5 lives.

Each bullet you get hit by takes away 1 of your lives. 

The game continues until you lose all 5 lives, which is when the game is over.

Enjoy!
